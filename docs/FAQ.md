# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I set up my AI Call Assistant for the first time?

For full phone integration, you'll need to connect your business phone number through Twilio integration.

### Q: Do I need any special equipment or software?
**A:** No special equipment needed. You only need:
- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- Your business phone number (for call routing)
- Mobile device (optional, for the mobile app)

### Q: How long does setup take?
**A:** Initial setup takes 5-10 minutes:
- Dashboard access: Immediate
- Basic configuration: 5 minutes
- Contact import: 2-5 minutes
- Phone integration: Contact support for assistance

## Contact Management

### Q: How do I import my existing contacts?
**A:** Three ways to import contacts:

1. **Mobile App Sync:**
   - Install the mobile app
   - Grant contact permissions
   - Tap "Sync Contacts"

2. **File Upload:**
   - Export contacts as CSV or VCF
   - Use "Import Contacts" in dashboard
   - Map fields and confirm

3. **Manual Entry:**
   - Add contacts one by one
   - Use "Add Contact" button
   - Enter required information

### Q: What contact information should I include?
**A:** Required fields:
- First Name
- Last Name
- Phone Number (format: +1234567890)

Optional but recommended:
- Email address
- Company name
- Job title
- VIP status

### Q: How do I mark someone as a VIP contact?
**A:** 
1. Open the contact in your contact list
2. Click "Edit Contact"
3. Check the "VIP" checkbox
4. Save changes

VIP contacts receive priority handling and immediate notifications.

### Q: Can I sync contacts from multiple devices?
**A:** Yes, you can sync from multiple devices:
- Each device gets a unique sync ID
- Contacts are merged intelligently
- Duplicates are automatically detected
- All syncs are encrypted and secure

## Call Routing

### Q: How does the AI decide where to route calls?
**A:** The AI uses multiple factors:
1. **Caller Identity:** Known contacts get personalized routing
2. **Keywords:** Specific words trigger routing rules
3. **Intent Analysis:** AI understands caller's purpose
4. **Time Conditions:** Business hours affect routing
5. **Priority Rules:** VIP contacts get special handling

### Q: Can I create custom routing rules?
**A:** Yes, you can create detailed routing rules:

**For All Calls:**
- Go to "Call Routes" section
- Click "Add Route"
- Set conditions (keywords, time, caller type)
- Choose action (forward, AI, voicemail, block)

**For Specific Contacts:**
- Open contact details
- Click "Add Routing Rule"
- Set contact-specific actions
- Assign priority level

### Q: What happens if no routing rule matches?
**A:** Default behavior:
- AI assistant answers the call
- Attempts to understand caller intent
- Provides helpful responses
- Offers voicemail option
- Can transfer to available numbers

### Q: How do I test my routing rules?
**A:** Several testing options:
1. Use the "Test Call" feature in settings
2. Call your number from different phones
3. Check the call logs for routing decisions
4. Review AI responses and outcomes
