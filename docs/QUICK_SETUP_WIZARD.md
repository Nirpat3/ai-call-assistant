# Quick Setup Wizard: AI Call Assistant

## 🚀 Get Started in 30 Minutes

Welcome! This wizard will guide you through setting up your AI Call Assistant. Follow each step in order for the smoothest experience.

---

## ⏱️ Time Estimate
- **Immediate Setup**: 30 minutes (voice calls work right away)
- **SMS Setup**: 3-4 weeks (requires government registration)

---

## 📋 Pre-Setup Checklist

Before we begin, make sure you have:

### Required Information
- ✅ Business name (as registered with government)
- ✅ Business address (physical location)
- ✅ Tax ID (EIN or SSN)
- ✅ Business email address
- ✅ Credit card for fees (~$59)

### Required Documents
- ✅ Business license or incorporation papers
- ✅ Government-issued photo ID
- ✅ Proof of business address

## 🎯 Setup Steps

### Step 1: Platform Account Setup
**Time: 5 minutes**

1. **Create Account**
   - Enter business name: `[Your Business Name]`
   - Your name and title
   - Business email
   - Secure password

2. **Verify Email**
   - Check your inbox
   - Click verification link
   - Log back in

3. **Complete Profile**
   - Upload logo (optional)
   - Set business hours
   - Choose time zone
   - Save profile

**✅ Checkpoint**: You should now be logged into your dashboard

---

### Step 2: Twilio Account Creation
**Time: 10 minutes**

1. **Sign Up for Twilio**
   - Go to: [twilio.com/try-twilio](https://www.twilio.com/try-twilio)
   - Use your business email
   - Verify with your personal phone
   - Complete account verification

2. **Upgrade Account**
   - Click "Upgrade your account"
   - Add credit card
   - Add $20 minimum balance
   - Account is now live (no trial restrictions)

3. **Get Your Credentials**
   - Go to Console → Settings → General
   - Copy and save securely:
     ```
     Account SID: AC________________________
     Auth Token: ___________________________
     ```

**✅ Checkpoint**: You have Twilio credentials ready

---

### Step 3: Phone Number Purchase
**Time: 5 minutes**

1. **Buy Number**
   - In Twilio Console: Phone Numbers → Manage → Buy a number
   - Choose "Local" number
   - Enter your area code
   - Select a number you like
   - Click "Buy" ($1/month)

2. **Note Your Number**
   ```
   Your Business Number: +1 (___) ___-____
   ```

**✅ Checkpoint**: You own a business phone number

---

### Step 4: Connect to Platform
**Time: 5 minutes**

1. **Enter Twilio Settings**
   - Return to your AI platform
   - Go to Settings → Integrations → Twilio
   - Enter:
     - Account SID
     - Auth Token  
     - Phone Number (format: +12345678900)

2. **Test Connection**
   - Click "Test Connection"
   - Should show: ✅ "Connected successfully"
   - Click "Save"

**✅ Checkpoint**: Platform connected to Twilio

---

### Step 5: Configure AI Assistant
**Time: 10 minutes**

1. **Business Information**
   - Go to AI Settings → Assistant Configuration
   - Enter:
     ```
     Business Name: [Your Business]
     Description: [What you do]
     Services: [Main services offered]
     ```

2. **Greeting Style**
   - Choose tone: Professional / Friendly / Casual
   - Business hours greeting: Yes/No
   - Special instructions: [Industry-specific notes]

3. **Common Questions**
   - Add 3-5 questions customers often ask
   - Add appropriate responses
   - Save configuration

**✅ Checkpoint**: AI knows about your business

---

### Step 6: Test Your Setup
**Time: 5 minutes**

1. **Make Test Call**
   - Call your new business number
   - AI should answer with your greeting
   - Try asking a common question
   - Hang up

2. **Check Call Log**
   - In platform: Dashboard → Recent Calls
   - Your test call should appear
   - Review AI response quality

**✅ Checkpoint**: Voice calls are working!

---

## 📱 SMS Setup (Optional - 3-4 weeks)

For SMS functionality, complete A2P 10DLC registration:

### Week 1: Trust Hub Profile
1. In Twilio Console → Messaging → Regulatory Compliance
2. Create Trust Hub profile with business documents
3. Submit for review (1-2 weeks)

### Week 2-3: Brand Registration
1. After Trust Hub approval, create brand
2. Pay $44 registration fee
3. Submit for review (1-2 weeks)

### Week 3-4: Campaign Setup
1. After brand approval, create campaign
2. Pay $15 campaign fee
3. Submit for review (2-3 weeks)
4. Link phone number when approved

**Note**: Voice calls work immediately, SMS requires this registration process.

## 🛠️ Quick Configuration Tips

### Optimize Your AI
- **Be Specific**: The more details about your business, the better AI responses
- **Test Weekly**: Call your number regularly to ensure it's working
- **Update Seasonally**: Adjust business hours for holidays/seasons

- Check Dashboard → Analytics weekly

- **Wrong business info**: Update AI Settings → Assistant Configuration
- **Calls not logging**: Verify platform-Twilio connection
